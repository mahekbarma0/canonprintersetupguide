How to set up — Quick steps

Concise instructions for both wireless and USB connections. Follow the relevant column below for your preferred method.

1

Power on & prepare

Plug in the printer and press Power. Confirm it's ready (no blinking error lights) before proceeding.

2

Wireless (Wi-Fi) Setup

Enable wireless mode on the printer (press the Wi-Fi button until blinking). On the printer display select your home network and enter your password.

3

Download & run installer

On your computer go to ij.start.canon, enter your model, download the driver package, and choose Wireless Connection when prompted.

4

Confirm & test

Run a test print from your device to confirm the printer is connected and working on the network.

1

Power on & cable

Turn on the printer and connect to your computer using a USB cable (use a USB-A to USB-B cable if required).

2

Run the installer

From ij.start.canon download drivers and select USB Connection during setup. The installer will detect the printer automatically.

3

Install & verify

Follow on-screen prompts to finish installation. Print a test page to verify the connection and alignment.

4

Optional: Network share

If needed, you can share the printer from the host computer or add it on other devices via network discovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a CD to install the printer?

No — download the latest drivers from the official Canon setup page at ij.start.canon.

Why won’t my printer connect to Wi-Fi?

Ensure the printer and computer are on the same Wi-Fi network, confirm the SSID/password, and restart your router and printer before retrying.

Can multiple devices use the same printer?

Yes. Once connected to Wi-Fi, any device on the same network can discover and use the printer.

Where is my model number?

Typically printed on the front or top of the printer (e.g., TS, MG, or PIXMA series). Use this to download the correct driver.

Do I need ink to complete setup?

You can install drivers without ink, but printing and alignment require cartridges to be installed.

Common Issues & Fixes

  • Printer not found during install: Restart printer & router, reconnect to Wi-Fi, then re-run installer.
  • Poor print quality: Run printhead alignment and check ink levels.
  • Paper jams: Remove paper gently, check the path and rollers for debris.
  • Printer offline: Set the printer as default in system settings and verify network connection.
